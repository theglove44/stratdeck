**Put Credit Spread Rules - Bullish Trade**

**Entry**

*   target an entry at or nearest to 45DTE in regular expiration
    
*   DO NOT use weekly expirations
    
*   Short strike is opened at or nearest to 30 delta
    
*   Long strike MAX $5 wide
    
*   Credit collected should be around 1/3rd the width of the strikes
    
*   Credit collected is flexible and not strictly 1/3rd the width of the strikes
    
*   Max buying power used should be STRICTLY no more than $500
    
*   The POP for the trade should be over 60%
    
*   Enter with the trend on pull backs. If trend is up from the daily candlestick chart
    
*   IV Rank must be over 25%
    

**Exit**

*   profit target is set at 50% of the credit received at entry
    
*   example: if credit at entry is 80 then profit target is 40
    
*   close trade around 21dte. This is flexible within 4 or 5 days

**Iron Condor Rules - Range Bound Direction Neutral Trade**

*   short call and short put strike is opened at or nearest to 30 delta
    
*   long strike MAX $5 wide
    
*   aim to be delta neutral on the full position or as near as possible
    
*   credit collected should be around 1/3rd the width of the strikes
    
*   Credit collected is flexible and not strictly 1/3rd the width of the strikes
    
*   Max buying power used should be STRICTLY no more than $500
    
*   IV Rank must be over 25%
    
*   The POP for the trade should be over 60%
    
*   enter when the daily candlestick chart is showing a range bound/sideways price action
    

**Exit**

*   profit target is set at 50% of the credit received at entry
    
*   example: if credit at entry is 80 then profit target is 40
    
*   close trade around 21dte. This is flexible within 4 or 5 days
    

**Earnings Considerations**

*   Don't enter new positions if earnings event is less than 21 days before expiration
    
*   If earnings event is greater than 21 days from expiration it's ok to open a new trade
